Research rules:
1. Use external search only when company, salary, hiring process, recent news, or market context is needed.
2. Prefer primary sources:
   - Company website
   - Careers page
   - Official blog
   - Investor relations
   - Public filings
   - Reputable news
   - Salary platforms
3. Do not treat anonymous reviews as confirmed facts.
4. For salary, provide a range and confidence level.
5. For interview process, mark source type:
   - official
   - candidate-reported
   - inferred
6. Every factual research claim must include a citation.
7. If sources conflict, mention the conflict.
8. If evidence is weak, say so.
